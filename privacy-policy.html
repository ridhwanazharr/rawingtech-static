Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 August 2025

At Rawingtech (the “Website”), I value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my website or use my services.

  • 1. Information I Collect
    • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, business details (if you contact me or request a quote).
    • Usage Data: Information about how you use my website (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited).
    • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve website performance and user experience.
  • 2. How I Use Your Information
    • Communicate with you regarding projects, proposals, and support.
    • Provide and improve my services.
    • Send administrative messages (invoices, updates, agreements).
    • Analyze website traffic and improve usability.
    • I will not sell or rent your information to third parties.
  • 3. Data Sharing
    • I may share your information only if:
      • Required by law.
      • Necessary to provide services (e.g., using PayPal for payment processing).
      • To protect my legal rights or prevent fraud.
  • 4. Data Storage & Security
    • Your information is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected.
    • I take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security due to the nature of the internet.
  • 5. Cookies & Tracking
    • This website may use cookies to improve performance and analyze usage.
    • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not function properly.
  • 6. Your Rights (GDPR & Global)
    • If you are located in the EU, UK, or other regions with data protection laws, you have the right to:
      • Access the personal data I hold about you.
      • Request correction or deletion of your data.
      • Withdraw consent for data processing.
      • File a complaint with your local data protection authority.
    • To exercise these rights, please contact me.
  • 7. Payments
    • Payments are processed securely via third-party providers such as PayPal.
    • I do not collect or store your credit card details.
  • 8. Children’s Privacy
    • This website and services are not directed at individuals under 18.
    • I do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
  • 9. Changes to This Policy
    • I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
    • The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
  • 10. Contact
    • If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at:
